# DataBel Telecom Customer Churn Analysis & Dashboarding

An end-to-end business intelligence project that applies a structured **5-stage data analysis workflow** to diagnose and mitigate customer churn for **DataBel**, a telecommunications provider.

This repository contains the data analysis workflow, key business findings, and strategic recommendations generated from an interactive 2-page Power BI dashboard analyzing **6,687 subscriber accounts**.

---

## 🛠️ The 5-Stage Data Analysis Flow

To ensure data integrity and deliver highly actionable insights to executive leadership, the project followed a structured lifecycle:

1. **Data Checking & Validation:** Auditing raw data fields, handling missing values, and verifying data consistency across subscriber records.
2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Uncovering initial correlations, calculating baseline KPIs, and identifying high-level patterns in customer behavior.
3. **Visual Analysis:** Isolating critical drivers of churn by segmenting data by contract types, geography, demographics, and service plans.
4. **Dashboarding & UI Design:** Building an interactive, user-friendly 2-page Power BI dashboard ("Overview" and "Demographics") optimized for executive decision-making.
5. **Stakeholder Communication:** Translating technical data insights into concrete, high-impact business recommendations and retention strategies.

---

## 📊 Key Business Findings

The interactive dashboard revealed several critical friction points and risk areas across DataBel's customer base:

* **Baseline Churn Metric:** Identified a **26.6% global churn rate** across the 6,687 total active subscribers.
* **Competitor Pressure:** Aggressive competitor strategies emerged as the primary external threat, with **45.51% of lost customers** citing superior rival promotions, competitor marketing, and hardware/device upgrades as their reason for leaving.
* **Contract Vulnerability:** A massive retention gap was identified in subscription models—**month-to-month contracts suffered a severe 46.29% churn rate**, compared to an incredibly stable 6.62% for yearly contracts.
* **Geographic Anomaly:** Isolated a critical localized risk in **California**, where the subscriber churn rate spiked dramatically to **63.24%**, indicating potential regional service failures or hyper-localized competitor campaigns.

## 🖥️ Dashboard Previews & Structure

The final Power BI deliverable is structured into two main dashboard tabs:
* **Page 1: Executive Overview:** Tracks high-level KPIs, monthly recurring revenue (MRR) impacts, churn trends over time, and a breakdown of attrition by contract type.
* **Page 2: Demographics & Services:** Drills down into customer segments (age, region, group plans) and correlates churn with specific service subscriptions (e.g., fiber optic vs. DSL, international calling, and tech support add-ons).

## 💡 Strategic Business Recommendations

To proactively defend market share and maximize customer lifetime value (LTV), the following data-driven strategies were delivered to leadership:

1. **Targeted Contract Migration:** Deploy automated, personalized promotional incentives (e.g., discounted streaming bundles or device credits) to transition high-risk month-to-month subscribers into 1-year or 2-year agreements.
2. **Competitor Counter-Offers:** Establish a proactive retention desk armed with aggressive matching offers specifically designed to intercept customers looking to transition to rivals for device upgrades or promotions.
3. **Localized Market Audit:** Launch an immediate technical and operational investigation in California to diagnose the 63.24% churn spike, addressing potential network quality issues or matching regional competitor price-cuts.
4. **Loyalty & Engagement Programs:** Implement targeted engagement campaigns for long-tenure accounts approaching contract renewal, offering proactive hardware upgrades to eliminate hardware-driven competitor attrition.
